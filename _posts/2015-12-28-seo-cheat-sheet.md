<h2 id='Copywriting' >
SEO Copywriting?
</h2>
<p>
 SEO copywriting is the technique of writing viewable text on a web page in such a way that it reads well for the surfer, and also targets specific search terms.
<p>

<h2 id='ByPage'>
SEO by page
</h2>

<h3>
On-Page SEO 
</h3>

<p> It includes providing good content, good keywords
selection, putting keywords on correct places, giving appropriate title to
every page, etc.
</p>

<h3>
Off-Page SEO
</h3>

<p> It includes link building, increasing link popularity by
submitting open directories, search engines, link exchange, etc.
</p>

 <section>

<h2 id='Keywords' >
Keywords
</h2>

<p>
Use online suggestions tools, brainstorm for the best choices. 
</p>

<h3>
Frequency

</h3>

<p>
The number of times a keyword in a website title or description.
</p>

<h3>
Weight

</h3>

<p>
Number of keywords compared to the total number of words on that same page
</p>

<h3>
Proximity

</h3>

<p>
Placement of keywords on in relation to each other or to other words with similar meaning.
</p>

<h3>
Prominence


</h3>

<p>
How early the keywords is placed.
</p>

<h3>
Placement


</h3>

<ul>

	<li>
links, title and alt attributes
	</li>

	<li>
url
	</li>

	<li>
h1, title tags
	</li>

	<li>
html comments, meta keywords and descriptions
	</li>

	<li>
body
	</li>


</ul>

<h3>
Word Stemming
</h3>

<p>
Allows all forms of the keyword - singular, plural, verb form as well as similar words to be returned for a given search query.
</p>

 </section>

 <section>
<h2 id='Mobiling' >
Mobile SEO
</h2>
	
<h3>
Decide configuration
</h3>
	<ul>
<li>
Responsive
</li>
<li>
Dynamic serving
</li>
<li>
Separate URLs
</li>

	</ul>

<p>
For responsive sites inform viewport to search engines automatically by using:
< meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">

</p>

<p>
For dynamically served use:
<mark> Vary: User-Agent </mark>
</p>

<p>
For separate URLs:
Desktop:
< link rel="alternate" media="only screen and (max-width: 640px)"
href="http://m.example.com/page-1" >



Mobiles:
< link rel="canonical" href="http://www.example.com/page-1" >
</p>

<p>
